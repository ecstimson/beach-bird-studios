# Landscaping Web Design That Grows Your Business

## The Challenge Landscaping Companies Face Online

Landscaping is a visual business - customers need to see your work to trust your capabilities. Most landscaping websites fail because:

- **Poor photo quality** - Low-resolution or missing project galleries
- **No seasonal messaging** - Missing opportunities for spring cleanups, fall prep, etc.
- **Unclear service separation** - Mixing maintenance with design projects confuses customers
- **No outdoor living focus** - Missing the growing hardscape and entertainment market
