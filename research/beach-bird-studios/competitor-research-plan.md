# Competitor Research Plan - Beach Bird Studios

## Research Objectives
1. Identify top 10-15 competitors in web development/design market
2. Analyze competitor services and positioning
3. Identify content gaps and opportunities
4. Assess market positioning strategy
5. Recommend competitive advantages

## Competitor Categories

### Primary Competitors (Direct)
- Web development agencies in similar markets
- Figma to code specialists
- React/Next.js development shops
- Similar-sized agencies with comparable services

### Secondary Competitors (Indirect)
- Freelance developers
- Template/theme providers
- No-code/low-code platforms
- Enterprise development agencies

### Aspirational Competitors (Industry Leaders)
- Award-winning development agencies
- Thought leaders in web development
- Companies with exceptional client testimonials

## Research Focus Areas

### Service Analysis
- [ ] Web development services offered
- [ ] Design to code capabilities
- [ ] E-commerce specialization
- [ ] Maintenance/support offerings
- [ ] Pricing transparency

### Marketing Analysis
- [ ] Website messaging and positioning
- [ ] Case studies and portfolio quality
- [ ] SEO strategy and performance
- [ ] Content marketing approach
- [ ] Social media presence

### Client Analysis
- [ ] Target market focus
- [ ] Client testimonials and reviews
- [ ] Project types and sizes
- [ ] Industry specializations

## Deliverables
1. **Competitor Analysis Report** - Comprehensive overview
2. **Service Comparison Matrix** - Side-by-side comparison
3. **Positioning Recommendations** - Strategic insights
4. **Content Opportunities** - Areas for competitive advantage

**Research Timeline: 2-3 weeks**
