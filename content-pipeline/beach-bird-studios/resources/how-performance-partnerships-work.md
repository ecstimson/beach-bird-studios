3. **Market research** to validate opportunity and competitive positioning
4. **Partnership agreement** with revenue sharing terms and geographic exclusivity

**Qualification Criteria:**
- Established service business with growth potential
- Professional service delivery and customer satisfaction
- Geographic market opportunity and limited competition
- Partnership mindset and commitment to professional growth

### **Step 2: Professional Website Development**

**What We Build (Zero Cost to You):**
- **Professional website** reflecting your expertise and services
- **Mobile-optimized design** for customers calling from their phones
- **Lead generation forms** optimized for your specific services
- **Local SEO optimization** for your geographic market
- **Customer tracking systems** for performance measurement

**Timeline:** 3-4 weeks from partnership agreement to website launch

### **Step 3: Marketing System Implementation**

**Marketing Systems Included:**
- **Local search optimization** for "[your service] near me" searches
- **Google Business Profile** setup and optimization
- **Customer review management** and reputation building
- **Seasonal marketing** campaigns and promotions
- **Emergency service** optimization (when applicable)

**Investment from Us:** $10,000-$20,000 in website development and marketing systems

### **Step 4: Customer Generation & Revenue Sharing**

**How Customer Generation Works:**
- Customers find your business through our website and marketing
- They contact you via forms, phone calls, or scheduling systems
- We track all inquiries and customer conversions
- You provide services and build customer relationships
- We earn percentage of revenue from tracked customers only

**Revenue Sharing Model:**
- **You keep 85-90%** of revenue from customers we generate
- **We earn 10-15%** of revenue from tracked customers
- **No sharing** on existing customers, referrals, or your own marketing
- **Transparent tracking** and monthly reporting on partnership performance

## What Services Are Tracked?

### **Included in Revenue Sharing**

**We EARN percentage from customers who:**
✅ **Found you through our website** or marketing campaigns  
✅ **Contacted you via our systems** (forms, tracked phone numbers, scheduling)  
✅ **Hired you for services** within 90 days of initial contact  
✅ **Can be documented** through our tracking and reporting systems  

**Examples of Tracked Customers:**
- Customer finds you through Google search and calls tracked phone number
- Customer fills out service request form on website
- Customer schedules appointment through online booking system
- Customer responds to seasonal marketing campaign we created

### **Excluded from Revenue Sharing**

**We do NOT earn from:**
❌ **Existing customers** and their repeat business  
❌ **Referrals from existing customers** and word-of-mouth  
❌ **Your own marketing efforts** (truck signs, local ads, networking)  
❌ **Walk-in customers** who found you through other means  
❌ **Follow-up work** for existing customers after initial service  

**Examples of Non-Tracked Revenue:**
- Neighbor referral from existing customer
- Customer saw your truck and called main number
- Repeat service call for existing customer
- Word-of-mouth from family/friends
