## The Story Behind Beach Bird Studios

### **Founded on Partnership, Not Projects**

Beach Bird Studios was born from frustration with the traditional web development model. Too many great businesses - especially in rural North Carolina - couldn't afford professional websites, while others paid tens of thousands for websites that generated no customers.

**Our founder's vision:** *What if web developers shared in business success instead of just completing projects?*

This led to our **Performance Partnership Program** - a completely new business model where rural service businesses get professional websites with zero upfront costs, and we earn based on actual customer generation.
