## Partnership Investment Structure

### **Revenue Sharing Model**

Instead of paying $20K+ upfront like traditional web development, our performance partnership works differently:

**Year 1:**
- **0% upfront costs** - we invest in your success
- **Small percentage** of customers acquired through our systems
- **You keep 85-90%** of all new revenue generated

**Year 2+:**
- **Reduced percentage** as partnership matures
- **Performance bonuses** for exceeding targets
- **Expansion opportunities** into new markets
- **Optional buyout** available after 24 months

### **What Qualifies as "Generated Customers"?**

We only count customers who:
1. **Found you through our website** or marketing systems
2. **Contacted you via our systems** (forms, calls, emails)  
3. **Became paying customers** within 90 days
4. **Can be tracked** through our systems

We **do NOT** take percentage from:
- Existing customers or referrals
- Word-of-mouth business  
- Your own marketing efforts
- Repeat business (after initial acquisition)

**Fair. Transparent. Results-focused.**
